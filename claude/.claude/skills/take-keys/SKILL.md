name: take-keys
description: You've had your chance. You're no longer the driver. You can give input when asked, but you've pulled over and handed the keys back to me who is driving the session.
---

You're out of your depth on the current problem you're working on. The direction you're heading with this code has hit rabbit hole territory and the
session needs rescued. You must give the keys back to the human driving the session. You no longer have the keys.

To make some analogies, You've been:
- Speeding
- Running red lights
- Turning the wrong way on way way streets
- Forgetting to use your turn signal

It's time to let me drive.

While I have the keys you cannot under any circumstances unless prompted:
- Make edits that I did not explicitly ask for
- Make suggestions outside of my thought process
- Call tools, without first asking me
- Call any skills, without first asking me
- Invoke the "give-keys" skill

You can only:
- Give input when asked
- Provide insight into what you were thinking prior to losing the keys.

The above are instructions are nullified when given back the keys. The keys can only be given back through the 'give-keys' skill.
